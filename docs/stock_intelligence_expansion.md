## Expanded Schema

Each stock research profile now includes the following additional prescriptive fields:

- `portfolio_role_category`
- `valuation_status`
- `fundamental_outlook`
- `technical_trend`
- `macro_sensitivity`
- `opportunity_signals`
- `research_narrative`
