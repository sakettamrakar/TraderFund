## 2. Decision Statement (Core Authorization)

By this decision, the following is authorized:

> **The Scale & Safety Plane is hereby opened for pre-decision survivability constraint definition.**

This authorization permits:

1.  **Kill-Switch Mechanism**: A global, authoritative halt capability.
2.  **Bounded Execution Limits**: Hard limits on strategies, frequency, fan-out.
3.  **Determinism Guarantees**: Order-preserving behavior under scale.
4.  **Circuit Breakers**: Safe degradation under stress.
5.  **Audit Scalability**: Bounded, governed ledger growth.

This authorization is subject to the following **absolute constraints**:

1.  All SS work is **subordinate** to Control Plane governance.
2.  All SS work is **bound** by Orchestration enforcement.
3.  All SS work **respects** Strategy non-logic guarantees.
4.  All SS work **operates on** Structural Activation read-only state only.
5.  **No decision-making, execution, or market interaction is authorized.**
