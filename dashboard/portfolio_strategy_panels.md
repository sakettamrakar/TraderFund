# Portfolio Strategy Panels

## Panels Added to Dashboard

### Portfolio Intelligence Summary

Displays:

- resilience assessment
- sector concentration analysis
- macro regime compatibility
- factor imbalance detection
- portfolio strengthening insights

### Portfolio Strategy Suggestions

Displays:

- prescriptive suggestion headline
- concrete advisory detail
- confidence level
- synthesized narrative summary

### Opportunity Signals

Displays portfolio-visible stock opportunity signals:

- ticker
- signal category
- explanation
- portfolio role category
- confidence level

### Stock Intelligence Profiles

Expanded to show:

- role category
- valuation status
- technical trend
- macro sensitivity
- opportunity signals
- research narrative

### Risk Alerts

Continues to display stock-level and portfolio-level flags.
