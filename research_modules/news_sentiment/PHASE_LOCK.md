## Current Status

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Authority Level** | RESEARCH-ONLY |
| **Activation Phase** | Phase 9+ |
| **Current Phase** | Phase 5 |
| **Trade Decision Authority** | ❌ FORBIDDEN |

---

## Activation Requirements

Before this module can influence trade decisions, ALL of the following must be true:

1. ✅ Project has reached **Phase 9**.
2. ⬜ 60 trading days of passive sentiment logging completed.
3. ⬜ Correlation analysis between sentiment and price completed.
4. ⬜ Documented proof that sentiment adds edge beyond noise.
5. ⬜ Explicit sign-off in `docs/governance/ACTIVATION_LOG.md`.
6. ⬜ Promotion PR tagged `[PROMOTION]` and approved by Senior Architect.

---

## What This Lock Prevents

- ❌ Blocking trades due to negative sentiment.
- ❌ Boosting confidence due to positive sentiment.
- ❌ Event-based trade triggers.
- ❌ "News-aware" momentum filters.
- ❌ Auto-prioritizing symbols based on news.
